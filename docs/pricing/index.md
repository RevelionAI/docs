## Credit system

Credits are the unit of AI compute in Revelion. Every scan consumes credits based on the models invoked, the number of agents spawned, and the depth of testing performed.

- **10,000 credits = £10**
- A typical web scan costs 30,000–50,000 credits (£30–£50)
- A deep network scan with multiple agents may use up to 150,000 credits
- Credits never expire
- You only pay for what you use — unused credits roll over

## Compared to traditional pentesting

Traditional penetration testing engagements typically cost £15,000–£30,000 per engagement, take 2–4 weeks to schedule and deliver, and produce a static report that is outdated within months.

| | Traditional pentest | Revelion |
|---|---|---|
| Cost | £15,000–£30,000 | From £10 |
| Scheduling | Weeks | Instant |
| Delivery time | Days to weeks | Hours |
| Frequency | Once or twice a year | On demand or scheduled |
| Report freshness | Point-in-time | Continuous |
| Remediation tracking | Manual | Built-in |

Revelion does not replace every form of human-led security testing. For compliance-driven assessments requiring a named tester and sign-off, a human engagement remains appropriate. For everything else — continuous coverage, new feature testing, regression testing, client reporting — Revelion delivers faster and at a fraction of the cost.

## No long-term contracts

All plans are billed monthly. Cancel at any time. Purchased credits are non-refundable but never expire.
